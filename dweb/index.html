FAQs

Why come to Digitize?

Digitize is a hackathon specifically for curious high school students who are interested in entering tech fields, but have never been to a hackathon or have little exposure to computer science. However, even if you have experience, you are still welcome! It is a great way to learn about programming and interact with professionals in the field.

Do I need a Team or an idea?

No, you will have the chance to meet new people who you can choose to work with during the hackathon. Additionally, you can brainstorm ideas for projects once you arrive, and find a team.

What can I make at Digitize?

Anything you want :)

Can I leave early?

Yes, but you will need to inform us a week before by emailing us at []@digitizehackathon.tech. In this email, you should state the name of the parent/guardian who is picking you up and at what time you will be leaving.

What is Digitize?

Digitize is a hackathon for high school students in the Bay Area. It will take place overnight from September 22-23. It will be held at Medallia from 11am to 12pm.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24 hour period of time for students to develop unique computer science projects from scratch. Groups of around four students work together to bring their ideas to life with the help of professional mentors.
